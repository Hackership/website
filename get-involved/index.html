Get Involved

There are so many ways for you to get involved with Hackership, other than applying as a learner. Here they are:

Increase Diversity in Tech. Grants Progamme.

You can donate to our Grants Programme, supporting the promotion of diversity in tech and/or the study of a particular technology during the Batch. For instance, the Hackership has had a 40% female intake in each Batch, and we want to keep it that way, and improve this ratio even further! Please help us!

All grant sponsors will be featured on the bottom of our website (details in the form), as well as will be introduced to the Batch participants in which they sponsor

Encourage Learners

We are currently open for Applications! If you know an awesome potential candidate, encourage them to apply! Or maybe you have a website/blog/meetup where you want to spread the word? Please go ahead!
