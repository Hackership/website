Batch 15-09 Santa Teresa, Costa Rica

Sept - Nov 2015

After a summer in Berlin, Hackership sets sail for Costa Rica, where we will run a batch from September till November 2015. The batch will run full time Monday - Thursday for 9 weeks, including one reading week. Learn with us during the week and spend your weekends exploring the beautiful beaches and surfing the waves off Santa Teresa.

Applications will be processed on a rolling basis and are allocated in a first-come-first-serve-fashion. Apply quicly to secure your space.

Our partner in Costa Rica

Silicon Beach is a Costa Rica based forum for IT entrepreneurs & people looking to work, start-up or invest and network in a very special setting. Our goal is to inspire and bring together technology talent from Costa Rica and around the world to pursue their dreams or efforts in a more fulfilling and healthy environment (part-time, full-time, project based, etc.).

Success is typically driven by working hard--and smart. Success leads to happiness if we can balance the road to get there. So with today's technology and tools at hand, why not do so by working from an 'office' with a view of the ocean and taking a coffee break by surfing a wave--or recharging with a meditation class at sunset. We organize networking events and extend this into seminars, hackathons, workshops, coaching and mentoring programs as the forum grows. Entrepreneurs, innovators, coders, students, geeks, founders and business owners, please join us if you want meet like-minded people. Get help with getting started working from a place like Santa Teresa, participate in networking activities and co-founder mixers, find marketing resources and strategic partners, seek investors and venture capital or get access to advisory boards and mentors
