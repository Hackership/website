Applications

The Process

After you applied for Hackership, the Team will review your application and come back with further questions, if needed. Once all the basics are clexar and work out, we'd like to know you better. For which we'd invite you to join us for at least one Taste of Hackership (and make sure to talk to us and that we know your name). If you can't make it to any, let us know through the form and we'll schedule a meeting in person or via Skype-/Google-Hangout.

Timeline

Applications are open until June 1st 2014 23:59 CET. You will have a final answer by June 15th latest. The Batch starts July 1st

Emphasis

Batch-1 will have an emphasis on web-based multimedia. We have coaches ready to help on these topics and are very interested in learners eager to learn this field.

Batch-1

Where does it take place?

Hackership will be run in Berlin, Germany. You are required to be on site 4-days per week.

When does it start?

The Summer 2014 Batch starts July 1st and ends with a public graduation ceremony in the week of September 15th.

I want to learn something else than 'web-based multimedia' though

Though we have an emphasis on web-based multimedia, you can still apply with any topic you want to learn. We will as usually try to match you with other learners having similar goals and ambitions. Having an emphasis for us mostly means, we already have coaches for that topic ready and are looking for people interest in these topics specifically.

What does it cost?

We don't charge any upfront tution. Hackership is run on the idea that people acquire skills and with them are offered better positions and jobs. That's why every learner pledges at the beginning of the programme to support future batches of Hackership by giving 15% of their income after taxes for the following twelve month after their batch ended. Assuming they got a job or position in the field.

You can learn more about this on our blog.
