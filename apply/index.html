The Process

After you applied for Hackership, the Team will review your application and come back with further questions, as needed.

During the application form, you can also state that you are only able to make it if a supporting grant is made available to you. Learn more about grants here

Timeline

Regular Applications (cost 1800 Eur) are open and will close on November 22nd at midnight (Berlin time)! As soon as they arrive, we will review the application. If you applied before November 5th, you'll receive word from us by November 12th, otherwise we will get back to you before November 29th.

Once accepted, we expect you to transfer half of the tuition as a down payment to our account.

Hackership on Tour will run from 12th January 2015- 15th March 2015. It will be a 9 week programme.
