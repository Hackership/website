The Process

After you applied for Hackership, the Team will review your application and come back with further questions, if needed. Once all the details are clear and worked out, we'd like to know you better. For which we'd invite you to join us for at least one Taste of Hackership (and make sure to talk to us and that we know your name). If you can't make it to any, let us know through the form and we'll schedule a meeting in person or via Skype-/Google-Hangout.
