About Hackership

Hello hackership lets do some writing! At the end of the day, a good debate will leave you feeling inspired or educated (regardless of whether your argument won the day), whereas tech bashing often leaves the bashee feeling stupid, confused, angry, or all of the above. Enter the code school. For those who spend the last three years on Mars: code schools are institutions that promise to turn people into employable developers in under three months. During those months students will go through a set curriculum that will teach them just enough to get a job.

To all learners out there: Some people will have you believe that deciding on a language or framework is the most important decision you will ever make. It really isn't. Especially when you're just starting out, it's often far more important to go for a technology with a friendly community (so that you can ask questions) and to just get started! Don't fall for the 'I-will-start-writing-code-when-I-find-the-perfect-language' trap and don't let anyone else push you in it. Let discussions about languages be a fun past time, but don't let them derail you.
