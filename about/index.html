What Really Matters

Hello! Welcome to Hackership. We believe that life is a whole. lot. better. when it is jam packed full of curiosity and opportunity to discover.

Those moments when you feel the trepidation of a new challenge. The contemplation after a discussion introduces new ideas that alter and enrich your perspective. The wonder at questions you now have that you’d never even thought of asking before. The brain- ache of new information and experiences all coming at you just a little faster than you can process. And the feeling of quiet pride when you recognise a step change in how you’re thinking, and what you are capable of. These are the moments of connection and growth that enrich our daily life.

Learning is exhilarating. Yes, continuous learning is a crucial component of being a great developer, as the environment and technology around us changes so quickly. But it is mainly a crucial component of being a great and happy person too. We know learning shouldn’t stop when you finish school, university or have a certain number of years’ experience behind you. But also that it can be hard to prioritise learning without support from the environment around you. So that is why, for developers, there is Hackership. Our mission is to support developers to stay curious, and keep learning. In an environment that is supportive, fun, open-minded and accessible.

Education Values, Not Profit

Hackership is a value led initiative. Education should be affordable and accessible, not about profits. We are about learning and empowering everyone to become a learner. Individual. Life-long. Self-directed. These values shape everything we do.

We do, and will always strive to:
  • Publish our talks and resources open source and online.
  • Support the tech community wherever we run batches, for example through free and open talks and workshops.
  • Support and Increase Diversity in tech.
  • Keep the programme fee as affordable as possible.
  • Offer grants and needs-based programme fee loans.

This is all made possible by our amazing community support and unique structure. Hackership is a grass roots community initiative, where many talented people and organisations volunteer their time, incredible expertise and resources. (If you were to pay all of our people and space costs at the market rate, the programme would easily cost over 15 000 Eur per participant, and therefore, not exist.) We do this because we love it.

Our Legal Structure

We are currently registered as a UG, so that we can legally receive payments. However, we do not make, nor do we desire to make any profits from running this initiative. Were we to, these are legally bound to be directed back into the OpenTechSchool, where we hail from.
