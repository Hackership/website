Hackership Inhouse

Aside from our 9-week batches we also run inhouse trainings for companies.

The ability to code benefits not just developers, but other members of your organisation as well. We provide the training necessary to get them from zero to finishing their first project - Hackership style! Next to that we offer advanced trainings for experienced developers who want to challenge themselves and learn something new.

All our programs are based on our self-directed model, but can be tailored to your needs and desired outcome. Previous clients include Soundcloud and Qwinix Technologies.

Please reach out to us for more information and pricing: team@hackership.org.
