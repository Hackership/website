What to Expect

This is a self-directed learning programme. You apply with a technology learning goal and a project that you want to carry out, and we provide skilled, relevant Hackers to coach and support you, whilst learning.
This puts you in control of the speed and direction of your learning, and focuses on ‘learning through building’ because we believe that this is how people best learn. (Rather than simply working through a pre- defined set of ‘build-it tutorials’.)

Programme information

The process is Iterative: You apply to Hackership with learning goals (and also ideally a technology and framework focus, and a project you want to work on.)
During the Application Process you will develop a draft project plan for the time period.

Project Plan Reviewed by our Coaches. Feedback and Support to finalise it is given. Start Learning! As you go through the programme, expect to refer back to, and occasionally readjust your roadmap.
In addition, we provide a number of structures to best help facilitate and enhance your learning:

  • Each Monday: A planning Breakfast where every learner details what they will work on that week.

  • Each Day: A 10minute standup meeting with the whole group. Every learner lists their plan for the day, and whether or not they acheived their goals from the previous day. Suggestions to pair or meet with a particular coach on a problem are made accordingly

  • Each Tuesday: A slot for a skill share workshop, from either coaches or learners

  • Each Wednesday: A group lunch for sharing and problem shooting

  • Each Thursday: Demo time. Every learner presents on what they have studied, learnt and built that week, showing the code, and the progress their project has made.

  • Every 1-2 weeks: Evening workshops and extras, also open to the public Anytime: ask a coach for help! Code Reviews, Pair Programming, CV Consultation and Practice Interviews. Workshops on technical and non-technical developer skills
